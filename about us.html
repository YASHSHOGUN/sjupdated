Welcome to SJ Interiors DnD

Designs and Decors


At SJ Interiors DnD, we believe that every space has a story to tell. Our mission is to craft environments that speak to the heart of that story through thoughtful, tailored design. Whether it's a home, an office, or a commercial space, we focus on creating interiors that are as unique as the people who live or work in them.

Founded by Kanuri Sai Kumar and Rani Swapna Reddy, SJ Interiors DnD blends creative vision with expert execution. Together, they lead a team committed to turning your vision into reality, ensuring that every project embodies both aesthetic beauty and practical functionality.





Meet Our Founders

Kanuri Sai Kumar

As a specialist in interior architecture and design, Kanuri Sai Kumar is known for blending timeless elegance with modern innovation. His approach to design emphasizes originality and sophistication, ensuring that every space is visually captivating. With a keen eye for detail, Kanuri leads the creative side of the studio, developing personalized design concepts that reflect the unique style and personality of each client.

Rani Swapna Reddy

Complementing Kanuri's creative expertise, Rani Swapna Reddy is a master of project management. She ensures that each project is executed with precision and efficiency, overseeing the practical aspects of design, from budgeting to timelines. Rani's deep commitment to client satisfaction and her ability to translate creative concepts into actionable plans make her an invaluable part of the team. She ensures that projects are completed on time, on budget, and to the highest standards of quality.

Together, Kanuri Sai Kumar and Rani Swapna Reddy form a dynamic partnership that drives SJ Interiors DnD. Their combined expertise ensures that every project, regardless of scale, is approached with both artistic flair and operational efficiency.



Our Design Approach

At SJ Interiors DnD, we believe that collaboration is the foundation of great design. Our process begins with a deep dive into understanding your vision, style, and needs. We work closely with you at every step of the journey, ensuring that the final result is a reflection of your unique personality and lifestyle.

Our design process is both creative and systematic. From the initial concept to the final reveal, we guide you through each stage with clear communication and attention to every detail. Whether it's a full home renovation, a commercial redesign, or a single room makeover, we take pride in creating environments that are not only beautiful but also functional and comfortable.




Let’s Bring Your Vision to Life

At SJ Interiors DnD, we don’t just design spaces—we create environments that inspire and elevate everyday living. We would be honored to partner with you on your design journey, bringing your vision to life with creativity, precision, and care. Contact us today to schedule a consultation, and let’s create a space that feels like home.
